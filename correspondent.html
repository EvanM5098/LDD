3. How It Works

We combine manual precision with smart technology to give you accurate, up-to-date tracking throughout the document lifecycle:

Document Capture: Every document is scanned into our system and assigned a unique reference number with a QR code for fast identification.

Agent Uploads: Our mobile app enables agents to capture and upload PDF images of documents at every key stage.

Live Tracking: As documents move through the system, your dashboard updates instantly—so you always know what’s happening.

Automated Query Flagging: The system notifies you immediately when a query is lodged, helping you take quick, corrective action and avoid delays.
